	{2024 -- Present}
	
	{\em Caltech's Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowship (SURF)}

	{\em Mentors: Nils Vu, Mark Scheel, Saul Teukolsky}

	Developed a root-finding scheme that controls simulation parameters using Broyden's method.
